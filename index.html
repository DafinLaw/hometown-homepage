Visit Pascani

Enjoy the culture of the "Little walk city".

Top three activities usually done by the people of Pascani:

Cros running

When I was in School people often used to go every year to the charitable event called "Cros" to run and raise funds.

Walking through Esplanada

Every time of the day you would see people on the promenade area called Esplanada and chatting about diverse subjects.

Walking through Pestisoru

Besides Esplanada, this park is the second most visited place by the people. They really enjoy some walking around nature
